What sparked my interest in coding was my love for technology. For several years now I've been a thoroughly involved tech enthusiast. it started with smartphones. My very first smartphone was the iPhone 3GS, it was my first step into the newly arisen smartphone revolution after doing hours of research I decided to make the switch to Android.


I've been using an Android device ever since. Slowly I began to develop love for all sorts of technology and gadgets. After so long I decided that using and admiring tech wasn't enough for me so I turned to something even better, programming. It became obvious to me that I wanted to develop software for the devices I was so invested in. The first language I ever used was C#, as I was learning to develop it felt like a whole new world of possibilities and that is what I love about programming, you can do anything with it.
